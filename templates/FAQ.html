Frequently Asked Questions

What is SAFENET?

SAFENET is an innovative platform designed to detect and prevent malware, viruses, phishing attacks, and other cyber threats using advanced AI and security technologies.

How does the file analysis work?

Our file analysis system uses advanced algorithms to scan your uploaded files, generating a hash and comparing it with our database of known malicious signatures to detect threats.

What types of files can be analyzed?

You can upload commonly used file types, such as documents, images, and executables, for analysis. Make sure the file size does not exceed the specified limit of 20 MB.

Is my data secure?

Yes, SAFENET prioritizes user privacy and security. Uploaded files are handled with the highest level of encryption and are not stored after the analysis is complete.

What should I do if a threat is detected?

If a threat is detected in your file, avoid opening it on your device and ensure that your system's antivirus is updated to handle potential infections effectively.

Can SAFENET analyze URLs for phishing?

Yes, you can enter a URL into our analysis tool, which checks it against known phishing and malicious site databases to ensure its authenticity and safety.
